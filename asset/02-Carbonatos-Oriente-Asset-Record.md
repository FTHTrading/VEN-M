## 4) Expansion Project — "Ampliación de la Planta de Producción"

**Project Title:** Ampliación de la Planta de Producción de Material Pétreo en el Sector Cerro de Piedra, Municipio Ribero del Estado Sucre.

**Objective:** Full-capacity manufacturing installation to maximize production and supply non-metallic mineral demand in eastern Venezuela and international markets.

### Target Production (Post-Expansion)

| Product | Specification | Volume Target |

### Justification for Expansion

1. **Demand drivers:** Venezuela's economic restructuring is increasing demand in energy, agribusiness, petrochemical, steel, and electric sectors — all require limestone aggregates and lime.
2. **Strategic position:** Low operating costs + high-purity (96% CaCO₃) deposit = competitive international pricing without quality sacrifice.
3. **Underutilized asset:** Current 25% capacity represents immediate upside — equipment is the bottleneck, not the reserve.
4. **Social economic multiplier:** Primary sector activity stimulates employment and downstream production in economically depressed region.

## 6) Market Analysis

- **Construction sector:** Piedra N°2 is a direct-use material in concrete and structural fill — permanent demand tied to any infrastructure activity
- **Agroindustry:** Cal Agrícola improves soil pH for agriculture — growing demand in Venezuela's reactivating agricultural sector
- **Export potential:** High-purity (96% CaCO₃) limestone qualifies for export to Caribbean, Central America, and South America
- **Competitive moat:** Low-cost extraction (surface mining), proximity to eastern Venezuelan markets, and 44+ Ha reserve base

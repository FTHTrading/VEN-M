# VAULT SELECTION CRITERIA — CB ORIENTE PORTFOLIO

**Purpose:** Criteria for selecting and approving the third-party vault custodian for the Alexandrite collateral.  
**Asset:** 2kg Rough Alexandrite — appraised USD $42,000,000  
**Required By:** Lender approval condition at facility close  
**Date:** March 30, 2026

---

## 1) Why Independent Custody Is Required

The Alexandrite must leave direct Contributor control and be placed in an independent, lender-approved vault because:

1. **Lender cannot perfect security interest** without custody control — UCC-1 filing on moveable tangible property requires constructive or actual possession by the secured party, or a custody/control agreement
2. **Insurance requires custody** — All-risk specie policies at this value ($42M) require institutional custody, not personal possession
3. **Eliminates counterparty risk** — An independent custodian hired by the SPV cannot "disappear" with the asset as a bad actor individual might
4. **Bank-grade audit trail** — Professional custodians provide vault receipts, quarterly inventory attestations, and standard insurance certificates accepted by underwriters

## 3) Minimum Custodian Requirements

Any selected vault must meet ALL of the following:

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|---------|
| **Regulatory status** | Licensed and regulated money or valuables custodian in their jurisdiction |
| **Insurance maintained** | Own bailee / goods-in-custody insurance (proof required) |
| **24/7 monitoring** | Armed security; environmental controls (fire, flood, humidity) |
| **Electronic access log** | Full audit trail of who accessed the vault and when |
| **Quarterly inventory attestation** | Written confirmation of asset presence every 90 days |
| **Dual control access** | No single individual can access without secondary witness |
| **Lender acknowledgment letter** | Agrees to notify lender of any claim or removal request |
| **Insurance certificate provided** | Names lender as additional insured / loss payee |

---

## 4) Custody Control Agreement Requirements

The Custody Control Agreement between the Custodian, the SPV (Recipient), and the Lender must include:

1. **Asset description:** Detailed physical description of the Alexandrite; photographs; appraisal report reference
2. **Lender's rights:** Custodian agrees to accept instructions from Lender upon written notice of default; Custodian will NOT release the asset to SPV without Lender's written consent while facility is outstanding
3. **Access restriction:** No access to the asset without dual-party authorization (SPV manager + Lender, or Lender alone upon default)
4. **Inspection rights:** Lender (or its designee) may inspect the asset up to once per quarter upon reasonable notice
5. **Release conditions:** Asset may be released only upon: (a) Lender written consent, or (b) full repayment of all facility obligations
6. **Notice of claims:** Custodian must notify Lender within 24 hours of any claim, loss, damage, or legal proceeding affecting the asset
7. **Termination:** Agreement terminates only when (i) facility is fully repaid AND (ii) Lender instructs custodian in writing

## 5) Pre-Custody Inspection Requirements

Before the Alexandrite is delivered to any vault:

- [ ] **Independent gemological inspection** — A licensed GIA-trained gemologist (independent of the appraiser) physically examines and confirms the stone matches the description in Report IDH11022025-5432-2KG
- [ ] **Photographic record** — High-resolution photographs taken from multiple angles; stored by Custodian and provided to Lender
- [ ] **Weight confirmation** — Stone weighed on calibrated scale; weight recorded in vault receipt
- [ ] **SHA-256 hash verification** — Lender confirms SHA-256 `59B634D41C1B0913D63457955688AD4063CA7DDC73F6E5B25E7F18B7F57628BE` matches appraisal document on IPFS CID `bafybeihhqpgxb2lia2i6zwduk4mcqxtz37uzlnu73xwxinrkymwwhfkxny`
- [ ] **Vault receipt issued** — Custodian issues formal vault receipt naming SPV as owner and Lender as secured party
- [ ] **Insurance binder confirmed** — Insurance carrier confirms coverage is bound at moment of vault delivery

## 8) Selection Decision Process

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Request quotations from at least 2 approved custodians | Borrower counsel |
| 2 | Confirm lender's preferred or pre-approved custodians | Lender |
| 3 | Negotiate custody control agreement terms | Both counsels + Custodian |
| 4 | Arrange pre-custody inspection | SPV + Lender |
| 5 | Execute Custody Control Agreement | SPV, Lender, Custodian |
| 6 | Arrange transport and secure delivery | SPV + Custodian |
| 7 | Obtain vault receipt | Custodian |
| 8 | Confirm insurance is bound at moment of delivery | Insurance broker |
| 9 | Notify Lender of completion | SPV |
| 10 | Lender conditions satisfied — facility draw confirmed | Lender |

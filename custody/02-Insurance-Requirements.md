## 2) Why Specie Insurance (Not General Property)

**Specie insurance** is the technical category for:
- Diamonds, gemstones, precious metals, pearls
- Negotiable instruments, cash, bullion
- Fine art and collectibles of high value

**General property insurance (homeowners / commercial property) does NOT cover:**
- Gemstones at full market value in institutional custody
- Loss-of-value events (theft, mysterious disappearance, damage during handling)
- Transit
- International locations

**Only a Lloyd's of London syndicate or a major specialty insurer** (see below) can underwrite $42M worth of specie coverage. This is not available from standard US property/casualty carriers.

## 5) Loss Payee Structure

The **Loss Payee** clause is the most critical element for the lender:

```
LOSS PAYEE:

[Lender Name]
[Lender Address]
ATTN: [Lender Legal / Collateral Management]
Re: Facility Ref: [CB Oriente Credit Facility]

All proceeds under this policy shall be payable FIRST to [Lender] 
to the extent of its outstanding principal, interest, and fees 
under the Credit Agreement dated [DATE]. 
Any surplus proceeds shall be paid to [SPV Name] Asset Holdings LLC.
```

**Why first-position loss payee matters:**
- In the event of a total loss, insurance proceeds go DIRECTLY to the lender — not through the SPV
- Prevents the SPV from receiving funds and not forwarding them
- Standard requirement for any asset-backed lending where insurance is a credit mitigant

## 6) Proof of Insurance Requirements

The lender must receive, before close and annually thereafter:

- [ ] **Certificate of Insurance (ACORD 27 or equivalent)** — Naming SPV as insured, Lender as loss payee
- [ ] **Policy binder / Cover Note** — Confirming coverage is bound as of custody transfer date
- [ ] **Full policy (on request)** — Lender has right to review full policy wording at any time
- [ ] **30-day cancellation notice** — Insurer must notify Lender directly if policy is being cancelled or materially changed
- [ ] **Annual renewal confirmation** — Evidence of renewal to be delivered to Lender 10+ days before expiration

## 7) Insurance Covenant

Per the Credit Agreement, the following insurance covenant applies:

> *"Borrower shall at all times during the term of this Agreement maintain all-risk specie insurance on the Collateral in the minimum amount of $42,000,000, with Lender named as first-loss payee. Failure to maintain such insurance, or failure to provide evidence thereof within 5 Business Days of Lender's written request, shall constitute an Event of Default."*

**Consequence of lapse:** Any gap in insurance coverage — even one day — is an Event of Default under the facility. The SPV must manage insurance renewal actively.

Lender Controls

Five Independent Enforcement Paths

Physical Custody Control

Custody Control Agreement binds the vault. Lender consent required for any asset release. On default: lender exercises direct custody control without SPV approval.

Insurance Loss Payee

$42M all-risk specie policy (Lloyd's / Chubb / AXA). Lender is FIRST loss payee. Total loss → insurance proceeds flow directly to lender, not through SPV.

UCC-1 Perfected Lien

Security interest filed with Florida Secretary of State. Lender has legal priority over all other creditors. May levy and sell collateral per Florida UCC Article 9.

Account Control

SPV controlled account requires lender approval for draws above threshold. On default: lender can freeze or redirect account per security agreement.

XRPL Evidence Verification

Immutable on-ledger SHA-256 attestation. Lender independently verifies appraisal document authenticity without relying on any party. Cannot be manipulated by SPV.

Venezuelan Project Pledge

Pledge of equity interests in Carbonatos, Curaoil, and Fila Maestra concessions. Lender may enforce against project assets under Venezuelan law on default.

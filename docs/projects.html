04 — Thermal Coal

Yacimiento Carbonifero Fila Maestra

Anzoátegui State, Venezuela

Three coal mining concessions (Fila Maestra I, II, III) in the NW Anzóategui state municipalities of Bruzual and Peñalver. Historically operated by CAVOVEN (British Coal Mining / EPM) from 1978–1993 with accumulated production of approximately 1 million tonnes.

Modern infrastructure (KM 235 coastal highway to Boca de Uchire) allows road access for export via Guyana. The Venezuelan government proposed a €9M development program in 2019 via Carbones Guri Guri, confirming ongoing strategic interest.

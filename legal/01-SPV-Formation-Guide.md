## 1) Why an SPV

A bankruptcy-remote SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) is the standard structure for asset-backed lending because:

1. **Lender protection:** SPV isolates the collateral (Alexandrite + project interests) from the operating company's general liabilities
2. **Enforcement clarity:** A perfected security interest against an SPV-held asset is cleaner to enforce than a claim against an operating entity
3. **Waterfall control:** Cash flow waterfall operates through the SPV — lender controls sequence of payment
4. **Regulatory alignment:** Most institutional lenders and credit facilities require SPV structure for hard-asset collateral deals

## 2) Recommended SPV Structure

### Primary Vehicle

**Type:** Florida or Wyoming LLC (bankruptcy-remote, single-purpose)  
**Name (suggested):** CB Oriente Asset Holdings LLC or Danath Asset Holdings LLC  
**Purpose:** Sole purpose — hold the Alexandrite gemstone as collateral and participate in the credit facility

**Key governance provisions:**
- Single-purpose restriction clause (cannot engage in unrelated business)
- Independent director requirement for voluntary bankruptcy filings
- Lender consent required for material decisions
- Defined permitted purposes and investments

### Subsidiary / Operating Vehicles

The three Venezuelan operating entities (Carbonatos de Oriente, Distribuidora Curaoil, Fila Maestra concessions) should either be:
- (A) Assigned interests into the SPV, OR
- (B) Operating subsidiaries with pledge of interests to the SPV/lender

*Counsel must determine the appropriate Venezuelan law treatment for each entity.*

## 3) Formation Checklist

### US SPV (Florida / Wyoming LLC)
- [ ] Engage US counsel — Florida corporate law specialist
- [ ] Draft Articles of Organization with SPC provisions
- [ ] Draft Operating Agreement with bankruptcy-remote covenants
- [ ] Appoint independent manager/director (required for institutional lenders)
- [ ] Obtain EIN (Federal Tax ID)
- [ ] Open dedicated bank account (segregated from operating accounts)
- [ ] Draft board resolution authorizing SPV activity

### Asset Contribution
- [ ] Execute Asset Contribution Agreement — Alexandrite from Investments Danath Inc. → SPV
- [ ] Document transfer with bill of sale, chain-of-title affidavit
- [ ] Update custody records to reflect SPV ownership
- [ ] Execute No-Lien / No-Encumbrance certificate (see legal/04 template)
- [ ] Obtain legal opinion on enforceability of security interest

### Venezuelan Assets
- [ ] Legal opinion on pledging Venezuelan mineral concession interests
- [ ] Carbonatos de Oriente board resolution authorizing participation
- [ ] Curaoil board resolution authorizing participation
- [ ] Fila Maestra concession interest assignment documentation

**4.3 Reporting.** Recipient shall provide Contributor with copies of all custody statements and insurance certificates quarterly.

**4.4 XRPL Attestation.** Recipient acknowledges and agrees that the SHA-256 hash of the appraisal document is permanently recorded on the XRP Ledger. Recipient shall not represent to any third party that any document with a different hash is the original, authentic appraisal.

**4.5 Return Rights.** Upon full repayment and termination of the Credit Agreement, Lender releases all security interests, and Recipient is wound up or the Credit Agreement is terminated, the Parties shall negotiate in good faith for the return of the Asset or its cash equivalent to Contributor.

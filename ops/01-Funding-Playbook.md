## Common Obstacles and Mitigations

| Obstacle | Mitigation |
|----------|-----------|
| Lender unfamiliar with gemstone collateral | Engage broker with specialty lender relationships; provide custody/02 + insurance quote early |
| Appraisal credibility questioned | Independent gemological inspection at lender's request; re-appraisal at second opinion |
| Venezuela country risk | Emphasize US domicile of SPV; Venezuelan projects are secondary collateral; primary is US-held Alexandrite |
| Insurance premium too high | Test multiple brokers; consider lower deductible trade-off; confirm custody location |
| Fila Maestra reserve certification not done | Be transparent: reserve study is funded from Tranche 3; Phase 1 money goes to get it done |
| Lender requires senior Venezuelan counsel opinion | Budget $50K for Venezuelan legal opinion on mining concession pledge |
| SPV not yet formed | Start formation immediately — Florida LLC takes 1–5 business days |

## NDA Handling

Once a prospect expresses interest, send a **2-page bilateral NDA**. Key terms:
- Mutual confidentiality (both sides protected)
- Purpose: evaluating a potential credit facility
- Term: 24 months from signing
- Governing law: Florida
- E-signature acceptable (DocuSign)

After NDA is executed, send the full diligence packet per [ops/03-Diligence-Packet-Index.md](03-Diligence-Packet-Index.md).

## Follow-Up Protocol

| Step | Timing | Action |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | T+3 days | If no response, one follow-up email: "Did this find the right contact?" |
| 2 | T+7 days | If no response, attempt phone call |
| 3 | T+14 days | If no response, mark as "low-priority" and move on |
| 4 | T+30 days | May send one final note if new information (XRPL attestation completion, custody update) |

**Target:** 10 outreach emails per week until 3+ substantive conversations are underway.

## Executive Summary

Carbonatos de Oriente, C.A. (formerly Canteras Piedra Blanca, C.A.) is a Venezuelan non-metallic mineral mining company with a 44.10-hectare limestone concession in Estado Sucre. The company has been operational since 2008, producing high-purity limestone (96% CaCO₃) for construction, agroindustry, and animal feed markets. Currently operating at ~25% of projected capacity due to equipment limitations, the company is seeking expansion capital to bring production to full rated capacity: **600,000 MT/year** of rock and **1,000 MT/month** of lime products.

## Market Demand Drivers

1. **Venezuelan infrastructure reactivation:** Multiple sectors (energy, petrochemical, steel, electricity) require construction aggregates — direct demand for Piedra N°2
2. **Agroindustry expansion:** Venezuela is actively rebuilding agricultural production — cal agrícola demand tied to crop yield improvement programs
3. **Animal feed manufacturing:** Calcio is an essential ingredient; domestic producers require reliable local supply to avoid import costs
4. **Export opportunity:** 96% CaCO₃ purity is export-grade — Caribbean, Central America, and Colombian markets reachable from Sucre's port access

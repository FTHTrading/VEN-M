## Executive Summary

Distribuidora Curaoil, C.A. proposes the construction of **Base de Almacenamiento y Despacho de Combustible — Sucre**, a modern, large-scale industrial fuel logistics platform serving the northwestern Venezuelan coast (Estado Sucre). The 12.5-hectare (125,000 m²) facility will store and dispatch diesel and gasoline in bulk to transport cisterns, serving agriculture, fishing, industry, transport, and commerce in a region with chronic fuel supply bottlenecks. The project is designed to API and NFPA international engineering standards and represents a critical piece of regional energy infrastructure.

## Technical Design

The facility is engineered as a specialized logistics-industrial platform with:

### Storage Systems
- Primary and secondary containment systems (API/NFPA standards)
- Underground and above-ground storage tanks — engineered distribution plan per minimum safety distances
- Tank spacing compliant with international standoff requirements (tank-to-tank, tank-to-property-line)

### Safety & Environmental Systems
- Automated fire detection and suppression systems (NFPA compliant)
- Hazardous area classification (IEC/NEC zones)
- Environmental spill containment — primary + secondary containment
- Operational protocols: strict dual-control procedures

### Logistics Infrastructure
- High-capacity vehicle circulation (heavy cistern trucks — wide turning radii, waiting zones)
- Green buffer zones around perimeter
- Future expansion provisions within site footprint
- Controlled-perimeter security

### Standards Applied

| Standard | Application |
|----------|-------------|
| **API** | Tank design, inspection, and integrity standards |
| **NFPA** | Fire safety — suppression, detection, electrical classification |

| **ISO 14001 basis** | Environmental management framework |

## Regional Need & Justification

### Problem Being Solved

1. **Supply bottlenecks:** Fuel currently dispatched directly from distant refineries or through outdated, undersized storage — creating logistics inefficiencies, higher costs, and supply disruption risk.
2. **Obsolete infrastructure:** Many transfer and storage operations don't meet modern technical standards — creating accident, spill, and contamination risk.
3. **Growing demand:** Agricultural development, port activity, industrial growth, and commercial expansion in Sucre State are all fuel-intensive — demand growing faster than supply capacity.

### Strategic Value

- **Buffer storage:** Acts as a strategic buffer between refineries and end consumers — stabilizes regional supply
- **Shorter distribution routes:** Reduces haul distances for cistern trucks → lower cost, lower emissions, faster delivery
- **Modern compliance:** First ISO/API/NFPA-compliant facility in the region → regulatory leadership position
- **Social anchor:** Stable energy supply supports hospitals, schools, public services dependent on generators in the region
